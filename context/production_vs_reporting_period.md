# Production Period vs Reporting Period

Production period and reporting period are separate concepts at Charm Industrial.

- **Production period**: Calendar month; covers biomass processing and oil/char production at the Colorado facility. Biomass Throughput sheets use this period (e.g., March 1-31).
- **Reporting/injection period**: Covers injection operations (e.g., March 3 - April 1). Used for COBB tab queries, vendor delivery sheets, emissions calc sheets.

**Important:** Conflating the two causes wrong date ranges on biomass throughput and other production-side sheets. When filling a Biomass Throughput sheet, always use the calendar month, not the injection reporting period dates.
